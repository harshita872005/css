Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya
  • Maida
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, maida, and a pinch of baking soda to make a soft dough
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Deep fry on low heat until golden.
  4. Soak in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water.

Barfi Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • Ghee
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Make jamuns using milk powder and fry them.
  2. Boil sugar with a little water to make syrup.
  3. Roll jamuns in dry khoya/barfi mixture.
  4. Garnish with chopped nuts or silver leaf.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashew nuts (ground)
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Cook sugar and water to make syrup, then add cashew powder.
  3. Stir until it forms a soft dough.
  4. Roll and cut into diamond shapes.
